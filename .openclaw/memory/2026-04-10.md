# 2026-04-10

## Range Balance Session

**Issue:** Jungle clan player reported range of 3 instead of expected 2 for their starting ranged unit (ranged_frame + basic_bow). Confirmed that basic_bow had rangeBonus=1 and ranged_frame had baseRange=2, totaling 3.

**Decision:** Lowered base ranges so only Catapult (and naval frames with no component) naturally have range 3.

### Changes Made

**chassis.json:**
- `ranged_frame`: baseRange 2 → 1

**components.json:**
- `musket`: rangeBonus 2 → 1 (infantry_frame+musket now = 2)
- `catapult_arm`: rangeBonus 1 → 0 (catapult_frame stays 3)
- `ship_cannon`: rangeBonus 1 → 0 (naval frames stay 3)

### Final Range Table
| Unit | Range |
|------|-------|
| Bowman, Blowgunner, Pistolier, Musketeer, Archer Cavalry, Mounted Archer | 2 |
| Catapult, Ranged Warship, Galley | 3 |

**Key constraint:** Catapult is the only non-naval unit that reaches range 3 through chassis alone. Naval frames (ranged_naval_frame, galley_frame) also have baseRange 3 and keep it since they use ship_cannon (bonus=0).
